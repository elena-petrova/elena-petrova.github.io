Guides

As we belive that everything can be learned online and for cheap, we are happy to offer you a collection of Data Professional Self-learning Paths. These guides sum up two years of personal experiences of learning Data Science from scratch and thus provide a budget alternative to expensive official courses.

The structure of the courses is organised in such a way, that the material can be succesfully absorbed by students with any backgrounds. As long as you are excited about your career in Data and motivated to study hard to achieve your goals, then this Self-Learning Path is for you.

All courses include:

  • roadmaps and milestones
  • timeframes for covering each topic
  • hand-picked and personally tested 100% free online resources
  • book to track your progress
  • motivational and mental self-help kits
